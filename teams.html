How it works

From first call to shipped capstones.

A team program is high-touch by design. Here's the shape of it.

Step 1

Scope

We talk through your team's level, your stack, and the roles you're hiring or growing into, then pick the track and tier that fit.

Step 2

Tailor

Modules are selected and the capstone is framed around a real problem from your world. Schedule is set to your team's timezone.

Step 3

Run

Live sessions, private channel, office hours. Each engineer builds week over week toward a capstone they present at the end.

Step 4

Report

Completion and coverage reporting for L&D, plus the shipped capstone repos as durable evidence of what the team can now do.
